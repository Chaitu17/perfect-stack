🎮 How to Play

  1. 👆
    Choose Your Vibe

    Pick from three stunning themes — Dystopian, Ancient Temple, or Deserted. Each has its own visual style and block colours.

  2. 🏗️
    Drop the Block

    Tap, click, or press Space to drop the sliding block onto the stack below. The closer to perfect alignment, the better!

  3. ✂️
    Don't Miss

    If you miss slightly, the block gets trimmed — your tower gets narrower. Miss completely and it's game over. How high can you stack?

  4. ⚡
    Perfect Stacks Reward You

    Land a block perfectly aligned and get a satisfying chord chime, a flash of colour, and an extra speed boost. Chain them for glory!

🏛️ About Perfect Stack

Perfect Stack is a free, browser-based precision block-stacking arcade game. No download, no sign-up — just pure stacking action. Built for desktop and mobile alike.

The game is inspired by classic stacking arcade titles, reimagined with modern web technology (HTML5 Canvas, Web Audio API) and three immersive visual themes:

  • ⚡ Dystopian — neon-lit cyberpunk cityscape with glowing cyan and magenta windows.
  • 🏛️ Ancient Temple — warm stone and gold tones evoking ruins of a lost civilisation.
  • 🏜️ Deserted — scorched terracotta reds and burnt orange under a desert sun.

Perfect Stack is developed and maintained by Chaitu17. It runs entirely in your browser — no tracking, no accounts, no data collected by the game itself.

Tips & Tricks

  • The block speeds up as your score climbs — stay focused!
  • Perfect drops keep your tower width intact. Prioritise precision over speed.
  • Use keyboard spacebar for the most precise timing on desktop.
  • The block bounces back from the edge — watch the rhythm and drop at the peak.

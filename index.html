Perfect Stack is a free, browser-based precision block-stacking arcade game. No download, no sign-up — just pure stacking action. Works on desktop and mobile.

Built with HTML5 Canvas and Web Audio API, featuring three immersive themes:

  • ⚡ Dystopian — neon-lit cyberpunk cityscape.
  • 🏛️ Ancient Temple — warm stone and gold tones.
  • 🏜️ Deserted — scorched terracotta reds and burnt orange.
